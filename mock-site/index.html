Pension Credit: eligibility

Pension Credit gives you extra money to help with your living costs if you’re over State Pension age and on a low income.

You can get Pension Credit even if you have other income, savings or own your home.

Who can claim

You must live in England, Scotland or Wales and have reached State Pension age (66 or over).

If you have a partner, you must include them in your application. You’ll usually be eligible if your weekly income is below £218.15, or £332.95 as a couple.

Around 800,000 eligible households do not claim the Pension Credit they’re entitled to — worth on average over £3,900 a year.
